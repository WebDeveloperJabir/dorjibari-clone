About Us

Welcome to Dorjibari

Established in 2006 by Md. Fazlur Rahman, Dorjibari has been a pioneering force in the Bangladeshi men's fashion industry. With a commitment to quality, style, and innovation, we have grown to become a beloved brand with 47 outlets across the country, offering a wide range of meticulously crafted clothing and accessories for men.

Our Journey

Dorjibari began with a simple vision: to redefine men's fashion in Bangladesh by combining traditional craftsmanship with modern design. Over the years, we have stayed true to this vision, continuously evolving and adapting to the ever-changing fashion landscape while maintaining our roots in quality and authenticity.

Our Products

At Dorjibari, we believe that every piece of clothing tells a story. Our collections are a harmonious blend of classic elegance and contemporary trends, designed to cater to the diverse tastes and preferences of our customers. From sophisticated formal wear to stylish casual outfits, our products are crafted with the finest fabrics and attention to detail, ensuring a perfect fit and unmatched comfort.

Our Commitment

Quality and customer satisfaction are at the heart of everything we do. Our dedicated team of designers, artisans, and customer service professionals work tirelessly to ensure that every Dorjibari product meets the highest standards of excellence. We are proud to be a trusted name in men's fashion, known for our exceptional craftsmanship and unwavering commitment to our customers.

Join Us Online

As we continue to grow, we are excited to bring the Dorjibari experience online. Our e-commerce platform allows you to explore our collections from the comfort of your home, with the same level of quality and service you expect from our physical stores. Whether you're looking for the perfect outfit for a special occasion or everyday wear that makes a statement, you'll find it all at Dorjibari.

Thank you for being a part of our journey. We look forward to continuing to serve you with style, quality, and passion.
